Social Science Study with VR

I helped the Virtual Embodiment Lab at Cornell University with a social science study in team building. For the research, we developed a mini game on High Fidelity (an online VR platform). In the game, two players need to jointly control a turtle to swim around and collect foods.

Travel Planning in VR

BonVR is a mobile VR app that helps users plan their trip based on their budgets and provides a sneak peek of their potential travel experience in VR. We designed and prototyped the app with Unity 3D in 2 days at the Cornell VR Hackathon. Below is a video demo of BonVR.
